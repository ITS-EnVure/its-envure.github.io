Ultra Fine Dust Removal

Fine particulates, also known as fine PM, Fine Particles or PM2.5. One of Major Harmful Atmospheric Pollutants due to it's ability to be absorbed into the bloodstream.

PM2.5 Removal

PM2.5: particles smaller than 2.5 micrometers in Aerodynamic Diameter. Due to it's low weight, it can travel thousands of kilometers before settling. This highly contributes to AQI and poor visibility.

TVOCs Removal

TVOCs: Total Volatile Organic Compounds


Organic Chemicals that have a high volativity at room temperature. Some are highly toxic and rapidly absorbed into the body. Major factor towards nauseaus and suffocating odors

Formaldehyde Removal

The only product in the market that reliably removes formaldehyde, a known carcinogen that may cause sinonasal and nasopharyngeal cancers. Short term exposure may cause oxidative stress and inflammation in animals.
